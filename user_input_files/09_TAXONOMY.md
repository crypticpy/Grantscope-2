## Maturity Stages (8)

| Stage | Name | Horizon | Description | Signals |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Concept | H3 | Academic research, theoretical | arXiv papers, university research |
| 2 | Emerging | H3 | Startups forming, patents filed | VC funding, patent filings |
| 3 | Prototype | H2 | Working demos exist | Conference demos, "proof of concept" |
| 4 | Pilot | H2 | Real-world testing (private sector) | "Company X announces pilot..." |
| 5 | Municipal Pilot | H2 | Government entity testing | "City of X announces..." |
| 6 | Early Adoption | H1 | Multiple cities implementing | Pattern of announcements |
| 7 | Mainstream | H1 | Widespread adoption | "Cities across the country..." |
| 8 | Mature | H1 | Established, commoditized | Industry standards exist |

## Triage Scores (3)

| Score | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Confirming | Confirms what we already know (baseline) |
| 3 | Resolving | Provides evidence for one of known alternatives |
| 5 | Novel | Suggests new possibility not previously considered |

---

## 7 Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Scale | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Credibility | 1-5 | Is the source reputable? Are there confirmations? |
| Novelty | 1-5 | Is this new to the world (not just new to us)? |
| Likelihood | 1-9 | What are the chances this will occur? |
| Impact | 1-5 | How big a change would this be? |
| Relevance | 1-5 | How important is this to Austin specifically? |
| Time to Awareness | months | How long until widely known? |
| Time to Prepare | months | How long before we need to act? |
